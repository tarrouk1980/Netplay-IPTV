## GDPR — Applies to ALL EU Markets
Every page imports `<GDPRBanner />`. All cookie consent, privacy policy, and data processing notices must comply with EU Regulation 2016/679. France (CNIL), Germany (DSGVO/BfDI), Belgium (APD), Italy (Garante) each have national enforcement bodies.
